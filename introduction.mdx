<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-2xl sm:text-3xl font-semibold text-gray-900 dark:text-white mb-3">Quick Start</h2>
  <p className="text-base text-gray-600 dark:text-gray-400 mb-8">Get up and running with Fitboard in minutes</p>
  
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Create your account">
      Register a new account with your email and password. You can also upload a custom avatar during registration.
      
      ```bash
      POST /api/v1/register
      ```
      
      <Accordion title="Example Request">
      ```json
      {
        "email": "user@example.com",
        "username": "fitnessuser",
        "password": "SecurePass123!"
      }
      ```
      </Accordion>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Authenticate and get your token">
      Log in to receive a JWT token that you'll use for all authenticated requests.
      
      ```bash
      POST /api/v1/login
      ```
      
      <Note>The token is automatically stored in a secure HTTP-only cookie for web clients.</Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Log your first meal">
      Start tracking your nutrition by logging meals with detailed macro information.
      
      ```bash
      POST /api/v1/meals
      ```
      
      Include meal items with nutritional data like calories, protein, carbs, and fat.
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Track your progress">
      Record body measurements, weight, and other fitness metrics to monitor your journey over time.
      
      ```bash
      POST /api/v1/progress
      ```
      
      <Tip>Set up regular progress check-ins to visualize your fitness trends and stay motivated.</Tip>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
